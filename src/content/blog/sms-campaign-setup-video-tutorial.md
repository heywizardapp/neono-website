## Video Tutorial

### Step 1: Enable SMS Marketing (0:00-2:30)

**What you need:**
- SMS-enabled salon software (like NeonO)
- Business phone number
- Client consent system

**Action items:**
1. Navigate to Marketing → SMS Settings
2. Connect your business phone number
3. Set up your sender name (shows as "YourSalon" on client phones)
4. Configure opt-in/opt-out automation

**Pro tip:** Use a dedicated business number, not your personal cell. This keeps work and personal separate and looks more professional.

## Step 2: Build Your Opt-In List (2:30-4:45)

### The Legal Requirement
You MUST have explicit consent before sending marketing texts. Here's how:

**In-person opt-in:**
```
"Would you like to receive appointment reminders and exclusive offers via text? We only send 2-3 messages per month and you can opt out anytime."
```

**Online opt-in:**
- Add checkbox to online booking: "☐ Yes, send me appointment reminders and special offers via SMS"
- Follow up email with confirmation link
- Auto-welcome text when they confirm

**Existing clients:**
Send one-time email asking for SMS consent with clear opt-in button.

### What NOT to do:
❌ Don't add clients without asking
❌ Don't buy phone lists
❌ Don't assume consent from having their number

## Step 3: Create Your First Campaign (4:45-7:00)

### Campaign Type Options:

**Appointment Reminder Campaign** (Start Here)
- **When:** 24 hours before appointments
- **Message:** "Hi [Name]! Reminder: [Service] tomorrow at [Time] with [Stylist]. Reply CONFIRM or call us at [Phone]"
- **Why start here:** Builds trust, reduces no-shows, familiarizes clients with your texts

**Birthday Campaign** (Week 2)
- **When:** 1 week before birthday
- **Message:** "Happy early birthday [Name]! 🎉 Enjoy 20% off any service this month. Book your birthday glow-up: [Link]"
- **Expected response:** 35-40% booking rate

**Last-Minute Fill Campaign** (Week 3)
- **When:** 4-6 hours before empty slots
- **Message:** "Quick! We have a [Time] opening today. Perfect for that [Service] you mentioned? Reply YES to book!"
- **Expected response:** 50-60% fill rate

## Step 4: Craft Your Message (7:00-8:30)

### The 4-Part Formula for High-Converting SMS:

1. **Personal Greeting** (2-4 words)
   - "Hi Sarah!"
   - "Hey Jessica!"
   
2. **The Value/Reason** (10-15 words)
   - "We have a last-minute opening..."
   - "Your birthday is coming up..."
   - "Ready for your quarterly color refresh?"

3. **Clear Call-to-Action** (5-8 words)
   - "Reply YES to book"
   - "Call us at [number]"
   - "Book now: [short link]"

4. **Signature** (2-3 words)
   - "- Lisa at Salon"
   - "See you soon!"

### Character count matters:
- Keep under 160 characters (1 SMS unit)
- Over 160 = charged for 2 messages
- Include emoji sparingly (💇‍♀️ ✨ 🎉)

## Step 5: Schedule and Send (8:30-9:15)

### Best sending times:
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** 10am-12pm, 2pm-4pm
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday evenings, weekends before 11am

### Frequency rules:
- **Maximum:** 2-3 marketing messages per month
- **Exception:** Transactional (appointment reminders don't count toward limit)
- **Sweet spot:** 1 marketing + unlimited transactional

### Test first:
1. Send to yourself and 2-3 team members
2. Check formatting on different phones
3. Verify links work
4. Confirm timing is correct

## Step 6: Track and Optimize (9:15-10:00)

### Key metrics to watch:

**Week 1 benchmarks:**
- Open rate: 95%+ (SMS opens are almost guaranteed)
- Response rate: 15-25% (aim for 20%+)
- Opt-out rate: Under 2% (if higher, you're sending too often)
- Booking conversion: 20-30%

### What to track in your software:
- Click-through rate on links
- Revenue per message sent
- Time from send to booking
- Which campaigns convert best

### Optimization tips:
- A/B test send times
- Try different CTAs ("Reply YES" vs "Click to book")
- Test message length (short vs detailed)
- Personalize based on past services

## Common Setup Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Sending without consent**
Fix: Set up proper opt-in process first

❌ **Too frequent messages**
Fix: Stick to 2-3 marketing texts/month max

❌ **Generic mass texts**
Fix: Use merge fields for personalization

❌ **No clear call-to-action**
Fix: Every message needs a specific next step

❌ **Wrong timing**
Fix: Follow the optimal sending schedule above

❌ **Not tracking results**
Fix: Review metrics weekly, adjust monthly

## FAQ

**Q: How much do SMS messages cost?**
A: Typically $0.01-$0.04 per message. Varies by country and carrier.

**Q: Can I send MMS (images)?**
A: Yes, but they cost 3-5x more. Save for special occasions only.

**Q: What if someone opts out?**
A: They're automatically removed. Never contact them via SMS again (but email/calls are still OK).

**Q: Can I text on holidays?**
A: Avoid major holidays (Christmas, Thanksgiving). Other holidays are fine with relevant messaging.

**Q: How long should I wait between campaigns?**
A: Minimum 5-7 days between marketing messages (appointment reminders don't count).
